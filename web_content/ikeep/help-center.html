How can we help you?

What exactly is Ikeep?
Ikeep is your personal "Where Did I Keep It?" assistant. It's a smart item location tracker designed to help you organize your home, track seasonal belongings, and remember exactly where you put that one tool you use once a year.
How does the automatic labeling work?
When you take a photo of an item, Ikeep uses on-device Machine Learning to analyze the image and suggest names and categories. This happens locally on your phone, ensuring your photos never leave your device unless you enable cloud backup.
What are "Still there" reminders?
For items you don't use often, it's easy to forget if they are still where you left them. Ikeep can send you periodic "Still there?" check-ins. You simply tap to confirm, or update the location if you've moved it.
Can I track expiry dates?
Absolutely! Ikeep is great for tracking batteries, documents, or even food items. Just set an expiry date when adding the item, and we'll notify you before it's too late.
What is "Lent Status"?
Borrowed a book to a friend? Toggle "Lent" on the item, record who has it, and set a return reminder. Ikeep will help you manage your lent items so they always come back home.
How do I safely backup my data?
In the Settings menu, sign in with your Google account and enable "Cloud Backup." Your data will be securely synced with our cloud database, allowing you to restore it on any device.
Does Ikeep support Family Sharing?
Yes! With Ikeep Plus, you can manage a shared inventory. This is perfect for households where everyone needs to know where the spare keys, passports, or camping gear are located.
