How To Request

This page explains how to request deletion of your Ikeep account and associated cloud data managed by Roy Digital.

  1. Send an email to roy@roydigital.in from the email address linked to your Ikeep account.
  2. Use the subject line Ikeep Account Deletion Request.
  3. If your Ikeep account uses a different email or display name, include those details so the account can be identified correctly.
  4. If you use Cloud Backup or family-sharing features, mention that in your request so the correct account data can be identified quickly.

Expected timeline: deletion requests are generally completed within 7 business days after identity verification.

What Is Deleted

  • Your Firebase Authentication account for Ikeep.
  • Your Ikeep profile document and account metadata stored for app operation.
  • Your cloud backup data, including synced items, synced locations, and uploaded item images associated with your account.
  • Your active nearby listings that are published from your account.

What May Be Retained

  • Support and deletion-request correspondence for up to 30 days after the request is completed, to handle follow-up or fraud-prevention checks.
  • Operational records reasonably required to verify and complete the deletion request, retained only for the minimum period needed.
  • Data already stored locally on your own device. To remove that data, uninstall the app or clear the app data from your device after your request is complete.

Important Notes

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
  • If you shared content with other users or households, some references may remain in other users' records where needed to preserve their data integrity or transaction history.
  • If you only want help with a specific item or backup issue, use Contact Us instead of an account-deletion request.
