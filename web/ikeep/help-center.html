How does Ikeep work?

You can save an item with its name, room or storage location, notes, images, tags, and reminder details. Ikeep is designed to make it easier to find things later.

Do I need an account to use the app?

No. Core usage can work without signing in. Some optional features, such as cloud backup or cross-device access, may require sign-in and connected services.

How do cloud backup and syncing work?

If backup is enabled for an item or feature, Ikeep may sync relevant data through connected backend services so your data can be restored or accessed on another device. Sync availability can depend on your plan, device status, and connectivity.

Does Ikeep store my photos and item notes?

It can, depending on how you use the app. Item photos, notes, and location details may be stored locally on your device and, where enabled, in cloud services used by Ikeep.

How do reminders work?

Ikeep can create reminder prompts for things like stale items, seasonal checks, due dates, or lent items. Reminder delivery also depends on your device permissions and notification settings.

What is Nearby Discovery?

Nearby Discovery is intended to help make lendable items discoverable within a broader locality area. The app is designed to use locality-level information rather than sharing precise GPS coordinates publicly.

Can I control what is visible or shared?

Yes. Visibility and sharing are intended to be user-controlled features. Review item-level settings before enabling backup, sharing, family access, or nearby discovery.

How do I update or delete my data?

You can edit or remove items directly inside the app. If you need help with account-linked or support-related requests, email roy@roydigital.in.

Why is a support page opening in my browser?

The Support section in Ikeep opens web pages hosted at roydigital.in so you can view updated help, contact, and legal information outside the app.
